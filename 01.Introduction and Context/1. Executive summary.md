# Executive Summary

This study case describes the migration of Identity and Access Management (IAM) capabilities to Microsoft Cloud (Azure AD / Entra ID) for a European energy and utilities provider. 

The initiative is led by a consulting team of three IAM experts:
- IAM Architect: Defines strategic vision and alignment with Zero Trust.
- IAM Solution Architect: Designs technical solution and integration patterns.
- IAM Business Analyst (author): Bridges business needs and technical delivery, ensures governance and process clarity.

The project aims to:
- Modernize IAM in line with Zero Trust principles.
- Reduce legacy fragmentation and improve operational efficiency.
- Establish clear SLAs to resolve IT chaos and restore business trust.
- Implement an IAM Center of Excellence to clarify ownership.
- Formalize a Shared Responsibility Model for cloud IAM.

This portfolio provides concise deliverables across project phases, demonstrating how IAM strategy, design, and governance are applied in practice.
