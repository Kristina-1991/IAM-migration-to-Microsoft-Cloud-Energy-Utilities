## Purpose
Define the target IAM architecture and operating model that will enable the company to achieve Zero Trust, regulatory compliance, improved user experience, and operational efficiency.

---

## Target Principles
- **Zero Trust by Design:** Continuous verification, adaptive authentication, and least privilege enforcement.  
- **Cloud-First:** IAM aligned with SaaS, hybrid, and cloud-native infrastructures.  
- **Automation Everywhere:** Lifecycle management for human and non-human identities.  
- **User-Centric:** Seamless, intuitive experiences for employees, partners, and customers.  
- **Compliance-Ready:** Built-in auditability, reporting, and regulatory alignment.  
- **Governance-Driven:** IAM Center of Excellence (CoE) ensures accountability and SLA enforcement.

---

## Future State Components

### 1. Identity Federation
- **Solution:** Microsoft Entra ID federation across cloud and on-premises resources.  
- **Benefit:** Single Sign-On (SSO), secure external collaboration, and unified authentication.  

### 2. Lifecycle Automation
- **Solution:** Entra ID Governance integrated with HR (SAP) and partner directories.  
- **Benefit:** Automated joiner-mover-leaver processes, timely provisioning and de-provisioning, reduced risk of orphaned accounts.  

### 3. Modern Authentication
- **Solution:** Passwordless authentication (FIDO2, Windows Hello), MFA, conditional access policies.  
- **Benefit:** Stronger security posture, reduced credential theft, improved user experience.  

### 4. Access Governance
- **Solution:** Entra ID Governance for access requests, entitlement management, and access reviews.  
- **Benefit:** Transparent workflows, auditable approvals, compliance with GDPR/NIS2.  

### 5. Machine Identity Management
- **Solution:** Integration of Entra ID with IoT/OT security tools (Azure Defender for IoT, CyberArk, Claroty).  
- **Benefit:** Lifecycle automation for SCADA and IoT devices, secure machine-to-machine interactions.  

### 6. Monitoring & Threat Intelligence
- **Solution:** Centralized SIEM (Microsoft Sentinel, Splunk) integrated with IAM logs.  
- **Benefit:** Real-time anomaly detection, proactive incident response, compliance reporting.  

### 7. User Experience
- **Solution:** Self-service portal for access requests, password resets, and MFA enrollment.  
- **Benefit:** Reduced IT workload, faster access, intuitive user experience.  

### 8. Governance & SLA Enforcement
- **Solution:** IAM Center of Excellence (CoE) with cross-functional representation.  
- **Benefit:** Clear accountability, SLA enforcement, continuous improvement.  

---

## Future State Architecture (Conceptual)
- **Core Identity:** Microsoft Entra ID as the central identity provider.  
- **Governance Layer:** Entra ID Governance for lifecycle, access requests, and reviews.  
- **Integration Layer:** HR (SAP), ServiceNow, SOC tools, SCADA systems.  
- **Security Layer:** SIEM (Sentinel/Splunk), Conditional Access, Threat Analytics.  
- **User Layer:** Self-service portal, passwordless authentication, SSO.  

---

## Expected Outcomes
- **Security:** Strong Zero Trust posture, reduced credential theft, faster incident response.  
- **Compliance:** GDPR/NIS2 readiness, auditable access reviews, least privilege enforcement.  
- **Efficiency:** Automated provisioning/de-provisioning, reduced operational costs.  
- **User Experience:** Seamless, intuitive access for employees, partners, and customers.  
- **Governance:** Clear ownership, SLA enforcement, continuous improvement via IAM CoE.  

---

## Conclusion
The future state vision positions IAM as a strategic enabler of digital trust, compliance, and innovation.  
By migrating to Microsoft Entra ID, implementing automation, and establishing governance, the company will modernize its identity infrastructure and unlock competitive advantage.
