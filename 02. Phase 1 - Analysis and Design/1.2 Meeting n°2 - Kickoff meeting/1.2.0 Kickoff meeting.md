# Kickoff Meeting – Validation of Project Charter and BRD

The kickoff meeting brought together the CIO, CISO, IT Infrastructure leads, Business stakeholders, and the consulting IAM team.  
The purpose was to validate the Project Charter and the Business Requirements Document (BRD), ensuring alignment across all parties.

### Key Highlights
- **Project Charter Validation:**
The CIO confirmed the strategic objectives: modernization of IAM, compliance with GDPR/NIS2, and adoption of Zero Trust.  
- **BRD Validation:**
Business stakeholders validated the documented requirements, including onboarding/offboarding SLAs, access governance, external collaboration, IoT/OT integration, and compliance reporting.  
- **Consensus Achieved:** All stakeholders agreed that IAM modernization is not only a technical upgrade but a business enabler.  
- **Next Steps:** Transition into Phase 2 – Design & Validation, with architecture diagrams, governance model, and roadmap preparation.

### Outcome
The kickoff meeting officially launched the IAM modernization program.  
The Project Charter and BRD were approved, providing a clear mandate and shared vision for the project.
