## Purpose of the Meeting 
The primary goal of this initial meeting was to establish a clear foundation for the IAM migration project by framing the project charter and beginning the draft of the Business Requirements Document (BRD). This session aimed to align all stakeholders on strategic objectives, key challenges, and the scope of work to ensure a shared vision moving forward.

## Attendees and Roles 
- CISO (Client): Provided insights into current IAM pain points and validated strategic direction.
- Chief Technology Officer (CTO) (Client): Contributed with a strategic view on technology alignment, infrastructure readiness, and cloud migration impact.
- Human Resources Representative (Client): Provided expertise on workforce processes and data quality related to identity lifecycle management.
- IAM Architect (Consulting Team): Presented the strategic vision emphasizing alignment with Zero Trust principles.
- IAM Solution Architect (Consulting Team): Shared preliminary ideas for leveraging Microsoft Cloud (Azure AD / Entra ID) as the target platform.
- IAM Business Analyst (Consulting Team): Facilitated discussions, ensured the capture of business requirements, and highlighted operational challenges.

  ## Agenda
  1. Introductions and clarification of roles.
  2. Presentation of current IAM challenges by the CISO and workforce identity consideration by HR.
  3. Strategic technology vision and cloud migration approach by the CTO and IAM architects.
  4. Review and discussion of the draft Project Charter with validation of strategic objectives
  5. Initial discussion on business requirements and operational constraints in preparation for the BRD.
  6. Definition of next steps and allocation of responsabilities
 
  ## Key Discussion points
  - Current IAM limitations highlighted: legacy systems, fragmented directories, manual provisioning, unclear ownership, absence of SLAs causing operational inefficiencies and elevated risk exposure.
  - Validation of strategic objectives: modernization aligned with regulatory requirements (NIS2, ISO 27001, GDPR), establishment of clear SLAs, creation of IAM Center of Excellence, and formalization of a Shared Responsibility Model for cloud migration.
  - Emphasis on preparing a clear, accessible BRD capturing identity lifecycle needs, authentication and authorization requirements, and governance processes for both technical and non-technical stakeholders.
 
  ## Next steps

  - The consulting team will finalize and deliver the formal Project Charter document.
  - Begin drafting the Business Requirements Document capturing detailed operational and governance requirements.
  - Prepare for the project kickoff meeting with a unified vision and documented agreements.

This first meeting established a solid foundation, transforming high-level ambitions into concrete commitments and setting the stage for a successful IAM migration project.
