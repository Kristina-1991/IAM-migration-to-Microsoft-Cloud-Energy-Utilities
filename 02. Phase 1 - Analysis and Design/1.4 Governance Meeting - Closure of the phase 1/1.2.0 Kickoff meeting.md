## Purpose of the Meeting
The purpose of the kickoff meeting was to officially launch the IAM modernization program, validate the Project Charter and Business Requirements Document (BRD), and align all stakeholders on strategic objectives, deliverables, and expectations for the project's next phases.

## Attendees and Roles 
- CIO: Project Sponsor, confirming strategic business aims and resources.
- CISO: Champion for security and regulatory compliance.
- IT Infrastructure Leads: Technical experts responsible for migration and systems integration.
- Business Stakeholders: Representatives voicing operational and user needs.
- Consulting IAM Team: IAM Architect, IAM Solution architect, IAM Business analysts, and project managers facilitating and guiding the transformation.

## Agenda 
- Introduction and clarification of roles and responsibilities.
- Presentation and validation of the Project Charter (scope, objectives, compliance).
- Review and endorsement of the BRD (requirements, SLAs, key processes).
- Discussion of project approach, governance, and collaboration framework.
- Open floor for stakeholder feedback and risk identification.
- Next steps and milestone planning for project design and validation.

## Key Discussion Points 
- Move forward to Phase 2 (Design and Validation), develop architecture diagrams and detailed governance models.
- Establish regular progress reviews, clear communication channels, and milestone tracking to ensure ongoing alignment.
- Assign responsibilities and schedule follow-up workshops to sustain stakeholder engagement through implementation.
