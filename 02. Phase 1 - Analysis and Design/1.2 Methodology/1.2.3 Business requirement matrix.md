## Conclusion
  
**Conclusion**  
This Business Requirement Matrix serves as a comprehensive and structured foundation for the IAM modernization project. It ensures that all business needs are clearly defined, aligned with applicable regulatory requirements, and mapped to specific IAM components and systems. By incorporating priorities alongside dependencies and constraints, the matrix facilitates informed decision-making, effective risk mitigation, and efficient resource allocation. It also supports stakeholder communication and governance by providing traceability from high-level objectives down to actionable requirements. Ultimately, this matrix guides the project team in delivering measurable business value, regulatory compliance, and a seamless user experience throughout the IAM transformation.
